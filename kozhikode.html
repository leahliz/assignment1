Peruvannamuzhi Dam

Peruvannamuzhi dam is a dam built on the Kuttiyadi river at Peruvannamuzhi in Chakkittapara Grama Panchayat, near perambra in Kozhikode district, Kerala.It's 55 km away from Kozhikode town. It was constructed as part of the Kuttiadi irrigation project. The reservoir of this dam is located in Chakittattara and Koorachundu panchayats. There are speed boat and tower boat facilities. It is a popular tourist attraction of Kozhikode district.The Peruvannamuzhi Wildlife Sanctuary is also known as Malabar Wildlife Sanctuary.
